About Us

Puppy Love is an online petshop based on Online Marketplace for dogs, pet food, accessories and other sevice. In order to accommodate the growing population of loyal and new customers who prefer to purchase via online, we offer a fun, fast shopping experience with wide selection of high-quality dogs and amazing customer service.

Mission

We are a group of KCGI Investors who came from various countries over the world. With our mission is to make dogs to be closer to human and give our customers the best online shopping experience by giving the best price and making each trasaction fun, fast & mobile-friendly.

Vision

To be the top of the mind trusted online pet shop nationwide.
